## How Does AI Achieve Precise Positioning?

TidySnap's AI can:

- **Identify item types**: Distinguish electronic devices, stationery, decorations, etc.
- **Analyze usage frequency**: Intelligently judge based on habits you describe
- **Consider ergonomics**: Ensure frequently used items are within comfortable reach
- **Provide visual guidance**: Arrows + labels, crystal clear
